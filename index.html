Problem Statement

I want to find an app that allows me to stay in touch with family and friends by checking their status with a single button. Often, it's hard to keep up with everyone through text and calls, so something simple that can remind me to check in with them would be nice. Something that can help me work through my mental health while still helping me keep up with my responsibilities would be the best option.

My partner sometimes struggles with overworking themselves during peak busy work/school season. I would like to have an app that allows me to send a quick check-up notification, just so they know that I am thinking about them. The notification should not be intrusive, but should just be a quick reminder that they should take breaks.

I have a family member that just moved to China. I have been trying to keep up with them through texts and calls but our busy schedules, not to mention the time difference, makes it hard. Through our limited conversations I feel as though they have been struggling with their mental health due to the new environment and the lack of friends so I have been looking for an app to stay in touch with them by checking their status with a single button.

Goals

  • Blend mental health checkups with a side focus on your personal daily responsibilities.
  • An app that works on both desktop and mobile without either platform lacking features.

Potential Obstacles

  • Users may have trouble finding a reason to use the app every day.
  • Users may feel overwhelmed by too many features.
  • The app must remain simple and easy to interact with.

Actions

  • Add events and tasks to a calendar with unlimited color sorting for subjects.
  • A simple button that can be clicked to send mental health reassurances and reminders (drink water, take a break, i love you, you're doing great).
  • Trackers for daily feelings, with the option to write about how your day was. Able to be shared publicly or privately
